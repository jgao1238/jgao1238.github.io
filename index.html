Bubble Tea Reviews

I love Asian food, but I don't like taking pics of what I eat because I just wanna eat. Look I'm hungry. That said, I consider myself a local bubble tea connoisseur. Below is a table of my personal verdicts on bubble tea spots near UMD.

Ranking Name Description Price Point
1 The Spot Mini Easily the best spot with a plethora of flavors and toppings. There's something here for everybody. $$
2 Kung Fu Tea A major chain across the US. There's a lot of variety here, but to me the quality just isn't up to par for what you're paying. Nothing to rave about but nothing to complain about. $$
3 Class 520 Nice peaceful dessert spot for ice cream and bubble tea. They make the tea fresh to order, so you get a yummy and cheap cup of tea $
4 Jumbo Jumbo The only reason why Jumbo Jumbo is above Ten Ren's is because of better sanitation. Bubbles are like rubber golf balls, and you can taste the powder of the tea. You also have to pay a bit more with card if you're below the minimum. $
5 Ten Ren's I'm not one to complain about food, but man this ain't it. If you're paying the same, you might as well go elsewhere for a better experience overall. Bubbles hurt your teeth chewing it, and you get a bit of tea with that bucket of ice. Food isn't bad, but place has been shutdown for sanitation issues before. $$
